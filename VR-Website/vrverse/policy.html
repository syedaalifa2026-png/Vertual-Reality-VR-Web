Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return for eligible products. Items returned after 30 days will not be accepted unless defective.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a return, the product must be:

  • In unused, original condition with all accessories, cables, and packaging intact.
  • Not damaged due to misuse, drops, liquid damage, or unauthorized modifications.
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt).
  • Returned with the original box and protective materials.

Non-returnable items: Digital downloads, gift cards, opened software/games, hygiene-sensitive accessories (face covers, foam inserts) unless defective.

Refund Process

  • Full Refund: For eligible returns within 30 days, we will refund the full purchase price excluding original shipping fees.
  • Partial Refund: Items returned with missing accessories or damaged packaging may receive a partial refund (up to 30% deduction).
  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we inspect the returned product.
  • Refund Method: Refunds are issued via the original payment method (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Visa, Upay) or Cash on Delivery refunds via bank transfer.

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery at support@vrverse.com with photos/videos of the issue.
  • We will arrange a free pickup and replacement or full refund including return shipping costs.
  • Manufacturing defects are covered under the manufacturer's warranty (if applicable).

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for the same product or store credit within 30 days. To exchange for a different product, simply return the original item and place a new order. Shipping charges for exchanges may apply unless the item is defective.

Return Shipping

  • Customer-initiated returns: You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • Defective/incorrect items: We provide a prepaid shipping label or arrange courier pickup free of charge.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method as we are not responsible for lost returns.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email our support team at returns@vrverse.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions (within 48 hours).
  3. Pack the product securely in original packaging.
  4. Ship to our return address: VRverse Returns, 123 VR Road, Banani, Dhaka 1213, Bangladesh.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the item (3-5 business days), your refund will be processed.

Refund Timeline

After we approve your return, refunds typically appear:

  • bKash/Nagad/Rocket: 1-2 business days
  • Visa Card: 3-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery (bank transfer): 5-10 business days

Sale & Promotional Items

Sale items are eligible for return under the same 30-day policy. However, any free gifts or bonus items must also be returned to receive a full refund.

Contact Support

For return assistance, call us at +880 1712 345678 (10AM - 8PM, Sat-Thu) or email support@vrverse.com. We respond within 24 hours.
